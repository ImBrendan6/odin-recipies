Potatoes and Greenbeans

Description

Simple delicpus potatoes and greenbeans!

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Green Beans
  • Seasonings
  • Olive oil

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F
  2. Cut potatoes
  3. Put potatoes in bowl with olive oil and desired seasonings
  4. Toss and mix together
  5. PLace potatoes on pan spacing them evenly
  6. PLace in oven for 20 mins
  7. Place green beans in same bowl and add seaonsings and olive oil and toss/mix
  8. After 20 minutes place green beans on pan and cook for another 25 minutes
